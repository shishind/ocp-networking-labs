🆘 EMERGENCY COMMANDS:

Service down:
  oc get svc <name> && oc get ep <name>

DNS broken:
  oc get pods -n openshift-dns
  oc exec <pod> -- nslookup kubernetes.default

Route 503:
  oc get route <name>
  oc logs -n openshift-ingress <router-pod>

Connectivity issues:
  oc exec <pod> -- ping 8.8.8.8
  oc get networkpolicy
